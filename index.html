about me

Hello! I'm Alasdair, a Web Developer.

After a first career spent in foreign language teaching I am excited to be making the jump to the tech sector. Now I am committed to perfecting my coding skills and combining these with my professional skills to create eye-catching websites.

My aim is to design and build custom websites for people and organizations who have passionate and innovative ideas to share.

I am based in Sioux Falls, South Dakota, USA, but I am originally from the UK. I am trilingual in English, French, and Spanish so I can easily build websites in all three languages no matter where I or a client is based in the world!
